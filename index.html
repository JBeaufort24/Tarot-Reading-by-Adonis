Who is Adonis Youngblood

Welcome to Tarot Reading by Adonis Youngblood. Discliamer

Readings I don't do:
  • ⛤ Health - Diagnoses and any physical health-related advice should always be left to the professionals.
  • ⛤ Finances - I don’t advise on any questions related to how or where you should spend your money.
  • ⛤ Gambiling - I do not do readings for lottery numbers, lucky numbers or taking chances with something that I don’t want to be a part of.
  • ⛤ Legal and Law - When it comes to the law of other countries, I don’t have enough knowledge to assess what to do, whether it’s legal or illegal, so count me out.
  • ⛤ Time and Dates - Don’t ask about “how soon”, “what time and date”. I'll never be able to give you an exact time or date. I can tell you what to look forward to and what to watch out for.

I’m a very straightforward person, I won't reshuffle my cards just because you don't like my answer, or it doesn't fit into your time frame or because it’s an inconvenience for you.
